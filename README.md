This is a clean and modern portfolio website built using HTML and CSS, designed to showcase your work and provide easy ways for visitors to connect with you. Key features include:

Google Map Location: Displays an embedded Google Map highlighting your location, allowing visitors to easily find where you're based or your business location.

Contact Form: A simple, user-friendly contact form that lets visitors send inquiries directly from the website. Fields include name, email, and message, providing a direct communication channel.

Google Calendar Integration: Embeds a Google Calendar where you can showcase upcoming events, meetings, or availability, giving visitors an easy way to stay updated on your schedule.
